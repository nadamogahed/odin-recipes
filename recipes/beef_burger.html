Beef Burger

Description

Need a basic burger recipe that you can whip up in no time? We have the perfect one. With just 5 ingredients and 15 minutes of cooking time, this flavorful recipe is ready to be shared, topped, and sauced in any way you like! Learn how to make your own sizzling beef burger patties right here.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground beef, egg, onion, bread crumbs, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, salt and pepper using your hands. Form the mixture into 8 hamburger patties.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate. Grill patties for 5 minutes per side on high heat, or until well done. Serve on buns with your favorite toppings.
