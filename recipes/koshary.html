Koshary

Description

Koshary is a traditional Egyptian dish that is a favorite for many. With layers of pasta, rice, lentils, and sauce, it's a hearty and delicious meal that is perfect for feeding a crowd. Learn how to make your own koshary right here.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 1 cup elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add lentils and cook for 20 minutes, or until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a separate pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add rice and cook for 20 minutes, or until tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a third pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add macaroni and cook for 10 minutes, or until tender. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and cook until onions are transparent. Add crushed tomatoes, vinegar, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. To assemble, layer lentils, rice, and macaroni in a bowl. Top with tomato sauce and serve hot.
